The game

Eye in the sky is an asymetric multiplayer game that can be played on either a desktop or tablet. The players can either play on the ground and try to collect all pineapples and bring them back to the home base before the time runs out or play as the Eye in the Sky who tries to interfere with the other players and stop them from completing their mission.
